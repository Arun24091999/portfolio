    Solar-powered soil moisture maintaning and irrigation System

    This system automatically irrigates crops by monitoring soil moisture levels using sensors. When the soil becomes dry, a solar-powered pump is activated to supply water, and it turns off once optimal moisture is reached. It uses renewable solar energy, reduces water wastage, saves electricity, and minimizes manual effort. The system is ideal for agriculture fields, gardens, and remote areas without reliable power supply.

    Wireless Power Transfer for Electric Vehical

    Wireless Power Transfer (WPT) enables electric vehicles to charge without physical cables using electromagnetic induction or resonance. Power is transferred from a ground-based charging pad to a receiver coil mounted on the vehicle. This method improves user convenience, enhances safety by eliminating exposed connectors, and supports automatic and dynamic charging. Wireless charging is suitable for homes, parking areas, and public charging stations, promoting efficient and user-friendly EV adoption.
